“My first experience in the field of Computer Science.”
I cannot forget my first experience in the field of computers.I joined a computer center but just for my summer vacations.Before that I used computers just for social networking. I had no intentions to join computer science in later years.But as I continued with computer sessions, it raised my interest computer languages.

I firstly started with computer basics course. After its completion, I randomnly, without any knowledge; started web designing course.On starting of web designing, at first, I used to get confused in usage of most of the tags.But later onwards, I found it very interesting to use and started playing with the codes of html and css by trying new things and tasks with them.

I made many webpages,templates and websites.Along with web designing,I used to try photoshop on my own.I made many projects in that also. It was my first experience in the field of computer science, after which I realised that I should opt for computer sciences as my future career.
